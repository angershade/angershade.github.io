A.I. Music & Angershade

There will be another Angershade album, and it will be released when I feel that it has reached a level of completion making it mandatory that the world hear and experience. And I’ve made my peace with the fact that the landscape is changing musically with the invention of Generative A.I. I don’t have any concerns for whether or not listeners will or will not dismiss it as another A.I. generated work of art. Who cares? It’s not important. What is important is that it sounds great when you play it loud, that it brings you fulfillment in whatever it is you need fulfillment in. And most importantly, that it sounds unlike anything you posses in your collection of music no mater how vast.
The goal of Angershade was and still is a project that represents an avant-garde exploration into sound design, deviating from the conventional methodologies of contemporary musical composition, production & engineering. And by that very definition A.I. is welcome here. A.I. has been with us for a much longer period of time than most realize. Your Air Conditioning unit in your home is a form of A.I., it’s goal is to simply regulate the temperature in a specific environment as it undergoes a series of stressors and relaxation processes when carrying out and having carried out said tasks. That makes it a form of A.I. that’s been around since the 1960’s and a very narrow one at that. A.I. is just a marketing term brought on lately by a technology that is really just an advanced search and assimilate technology. It can’t write music or generate art in a manner that allows it to reach deep within a soul it possesses shaped by a lifetime of trauma and defining events in its life. A mechanical being does not posses such things that a biological being does. Without a biological action a mechanical action cannot occur, the mechanical being will simply present a blinking cursor upon a screen and no more. This makes A.I. a mere tool no more than the computer’s that possess it and the programs that are installed on it. Much like the presets in a particular piece of software or plug-in, it is merely a jumping off point. The fine tuning through the sweet spots that are discovered are done from the soul for which only the biological can possess.
The level at which I utilize A.I. within Angershade remains to be seen and may never be revealed as I do not feel it would be at a level making reveal necessary. However I have not closed myself off to the idea of starting another project that will fully utilize A.I. and its capabilities in ways unimaginable. And if so, it would be disguised under a pseudonym not linked in any way to this website, Angershade, or ℗ Yegge. It could be your favorite band you enjoy listening to right now, it could be your most hated band, or something I have yet to start, or something I started long ago before A.I. was a buzz word capable of sending stocks skyrocketing to levels of profit so absurd. You will never know.

Get ready for a new website soon, it’s being meticulously worked on from the first line of code to the last.
